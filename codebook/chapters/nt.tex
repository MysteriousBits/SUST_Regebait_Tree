\codeWithDescription{CRT}{nt/crt.cpp}
[Computes the unique solution $x$ to a system of congruences $x \equiv a_i \pmod{m_i}$ using the Chinese Remainder Theorem. Assumes all moduli are pairwise coprime and the answer fits in \texttt{ll}. \\
\textbf{Usage:} Store congruences as \texttt{\{remainder, modulus\}} pairs in \texttt{mods}. Call \texttt{crt(mods)} to obtain the smallest non-negative solution modulo the product of all moduli. Requires \texttt{extended\_gcd()} and \texttt{modinv()}. \\
\textbf{Time:} {$O(k \log M)$}]
