\codeWithDescription{CRT}{nt/crt.cpp}
[Finds $x$ such that $x \mod m1 = a1, x \mod m2 = a2$. $m1$ and $m2$ may not be coprime.
Here, $x$ is unique modulo $m = lcm(m1, m2)$. returns $(x, m)$. on failure, $m = -1$.
]
