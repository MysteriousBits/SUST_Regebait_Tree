\codeWithDescription{Min Cost Max Flow}{graph/mcmf.cpp}%

\codeWithDescription{HopcroftKarp}{graph/hopcroft.cpp}
[Fast bipartite matching algorithm. Graph $g$ should be a list of neighbors of the left partition, and $r$ should be a vector full of $-1$'s of the same size as the right partition. Returns the size of the matching. $r[i]$ will be the match for vertex $i$ on the right side, or $-1$ if it's not matched. \\
\textbf{Time:} {$O(E \sqrt{V})$}]
